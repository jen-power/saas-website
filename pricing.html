Pricing

Our pricing is based on market demands, the cost of our service, and ensures musicians retain 100% of their commissions.

To learn more about how Rondo can serve you best, sign up to our service using the application here. As all our buyers and musicians are hand-picked by our team you will not be immediately registered for an account but will have full access to our communication team!
