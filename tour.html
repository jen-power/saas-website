Features

Features for Musicians

Musicians with Rondo are hand-picked by our team. As an elite member of the Rondo musician database, you can rest at ease when being booked for events, and enjoy the following features:

Make Your Own Schedule

Input your availability to inform potential buyers of when you can and cannot perform and what types of events you are interested in being a part of.

Set Your Price

You choose whether you’ll be paid based on the hour or a flat fee for the event. Include items on your roster for the day of the event.

Sign Contracts Online

Read your contracts with potential buyers, negotiate, and sign online without every having to leave the comfort of your home.

Perform

With all event and payment details secured through Rondo before the event, all you have to worry about the day of your performance is performing!

Features for Buyers

Buyers with Rondo are hand-picked by our team and include a wide range of promoters with previous experience in the entertainment industry. As a member of the Rondo buyer database, you can enjoy the following features:

Musician Databse

Access to a full database of musicians screened by our team at Rondo. Profiles include the type of entertainment, prices, and other important information about the artist.

Event Details

Provide all details related to your event, what type of entertainment you are looking for, location, equipment that is provided, and much more!

Secure Payment

Payment to musicians is all done through our app, ensuring that our musicians are paid in a timely manner and that buyers receive what they paid for.
